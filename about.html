Our Story

Harvest Market & Caf√© was born from a simple belief: that good food should be accessible, sustainable, and rooted in community.

In 2018, founders Sarah and Michael Chen opened our doors in Portland's Pearl District with a vision to create more than just another grocery store. They wanted to build a gathering place where neighbors could find fresh, organic food while supporting local farmers and sustainable agriculture.

What started as a small corner market has grown into a thriving community hub. Today, we partner with over 25 local farms, employ a dedicated team of 30 people, and serve thousands of customers who share our commitment to eating well and supporting local food systems.

Every day, we're honored to bring the best of Oregon's agricultural bounty to your table. From the organic greens harvested this morning to the bread baked in our ovens before dawn, everything we offer reflects our dedication to quality, sustainability, and community.
